PRINGLES FAVORITES



We know what you're thinking: "How can you have more than one favorite crisp?" But when it comes to Pringles, every crisp is your favorite crisp.




PRINGLES BBQ CRISPS

Snacking gets awesomer when you add the taste of BBQ to the mix. It’s the flavor that brings an outdoorsy vibe to any gathering, whether you're at home, picnicking or road tripping. Also, any chance to haul out your Australian accent and use the word “barbie” is a good thing.


PRINGLES CHEDDAR CHEESE CRISPS

We didn't use just any cheese flavor in Pringles Cheddar Cheese crisps. We went with cheddar - the king of cheeses. So you could even say it’s a royal flavor. It’s so majestically good, you might not want to mention it to your lactose-intolerant friends.




PRINGLES CHEDDAR & SOUR CREAM CRISPS

Imagine a baked potato smothered in cheddar cheese. Imagine sticking that potato under the broiler so the cheese gets all melty. Then take that imaginary potato and smother it in sour cream. That’s what these taste like.



PRINGLES JALAPENO CRISPS

For those who enjoy a good kick in the tongue, we give you a spicy flavor to excite your best buds (taste buds, duh). There’s only one way to find out if you can take the spice. It's a salty snack with a jalapeno flavor kick.




PRINGLES PIZZA CRISPS





Pizza makes people happy. Pringles makes people happy. Combine the two, and your party will be known as the happiest place on Earth. If morning-after cold pizza is your thing, store the can in the fridge.






PRINGLES ORIGINAL CRISPS

When you're an original, you set the standard for how it’s done. And with the tantalizing taste of potato you'll be taking this gold standard all the way to the flavor bank. It's the one salty snack that hits the spot every time.





PRINGLES SOUR CREAM & ONION CRISPS

Awesomeness of sour cream, onion and potato together can’t be measured by modern science. We’ve decided it’s simply a flavor combination nature intended and man perfected. We’ll just keep making ’em, as long as you keep nomming ’em.




PRINGLES RANCH CRISPS

The flavor of Ranch - it started as a dressing, found fame as a dip, then conquered the snacking world in a Pringles crisp. That’s just about the highest achievement a flavor can reach. Congratulations, Ranch! You're a Pringles flavor classic.




PRINGLES SALT & VINEGAR CRISPS

Pucker factor is just one of the many reasons to love these crisps. We took a little vinegar, a touch of salt and lots of crunch to make a flavor explosion in your mouth. And when you're done puckering, you can make duck lips out of them.



PRINGLES SCREAMIN' DILL PICKLE CRISPS

We say screamin’ not because we think that’s what you’ll do when you taste these crisps. We say screamin’ because the dill flavor you get is so big and bold and … freaking Xtra that it just screams “PICKLE.”




PRINGLES PARMESAN & ROASTED GARLIC CRISPS

Pringles is cooking up a new buttery, parmesan-packed crisp for 2020. Pringles Parmesan & Roasted Garlic brings to crunchable life a sweetly roasted taste explosion with the potential to ward off pesky vampires.



PRINGLES ROTISSERIE CHICKEN CRISPS

The most awesome part of a rotisserie chicken is its savory, roasted flavor. In 2020, new Pringles Rotisserie Chicken will slide onto store shelves with all the tasty, roasty, herby deliciousness you love in one crunchy bite.




PRINGLES HONEY MUSTARD CRISPS

Sweet and savory is what the people want. So we're giving it to them with a crisp that doesn't take sides. Pringles has it covered - with both Honey and Mustard flavor in one can. Life is good when you've got Pringles Honey Mustard flavored potato crisps.



PRINGLES BUFFALO RANCH CRISPS

Finally, a no-mess way to enjoy Buffalo and Ranch flavors anywhere, anytime. Pringles Buffalo Ranch has that tangy, smooth flavor, seasoned edge to edge.
